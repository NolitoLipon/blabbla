Boracay Island (Aklan)

Boracay island is one of my dream beach destination because of it's crystal-clear waters,
and boracay also is one of the top tourist destinations of the philippines.
Boracay Island is famous for its stunning white sand beaches, particularly White Beach,
which is known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant nightlife.
The island offers a range of activities,
from water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving to relaxation spots and local markets.
It’s also a popular destination for both tourists and locals,
providing a mix of leisure and adventure.

El Nido (Palawan)

El Nido is a perfect destination because of its dramatic limestone cliffs and turquoise water.
Known for its hiddden lagoons and snorkeling spots, its my favorite beach destination among the rest.
El Nido, Palawan, is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty,
featuring dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and stunning lagoons.
The area is a popular destination for island-hopping tours,
where visitors can explore hidden beaches, snorkel among vibrant coral reefs,
and enjoy the rich marine life.
It’s also known for its vibrant local culture and delicious seafood.
Many travelers visit El Nido for its serene atmosphere and picturesque landscapes.

learn more about elnido



Top activities in El Nido:

  • Kayaking in Big Lagoon
  • Snorkeling at Miniloc Island
  • Scuba diving at Bacuit Bay

Siargao island is on the list because siargao offers a a laid-back island vibes with stunning beaches.

Bantayan Island (Cebu)

Bantayan Island is a hidden gem in Cebu with calm, turqoise waters and long stretches of white sand.
It's perfect for a quiet retreat, away from the bust city life
Bantayan Island in Cebu is a popular getaway known for its serene beaches and vibrant local culture.
The island features stunning spots like Kota Beach, Santa Fe Beach, and the picturesque Virgin Island.
It's a great place for relaxation, water sports, and enjoying fresh seafood.
The island is also rich in history, with landmarks such as the old Bantayan Church.

Best things to do in Bantayan:

Camiguin Island

Known as the "Island Born of Fire", Camuguin is famous for its volcanic landscapes and stunning beaches.
White Island, a sandbar near the main island, offers beautiful sunrise and sunset views.
Camiguin is a small volcanic island located in the northern part of Mindanao, Philippines.
Despite its size, it boasts a rich landscape filled with diverse natural attractions,
making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
The most prominent of which is Mount Hibok-Hibok, Camiguin is a place of both beauty and history.
The volcanic activity has created stunning natural features, including hot springs, cold springs,
waterfalls, and lush forests.

learn more about camuguin



Activities in Camuguin

  • Explore White Island
  • Visit Katibawasan Falls
  • Sunrise and Sunset watching
